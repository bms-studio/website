Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

How long does delivery take?
Most digital products are delivered within 24 hours. Custom development projects vary based on complexity, typically 3-7 days.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers to BCA, BNI, BRI, Mandiri, and E-Wallet GoPay, QRIS, and PayPal. Payment details are provided after order confirmation.
Can I request custom modifications?
Absolutely! We specialize in custom development. Contact us through the contact form or chat to discuss your specific requirements.
Is there a warranty or refund policy?
Yes, we offer warranty on all our products and services. Each product comes with specific warranty terms. Contact support for more details.
How do I get support after purchase?
You can reach us through our contact page or WhatsApp. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
